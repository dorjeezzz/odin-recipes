Boiled Broccoli

Description

It's boiled broccoli! The healthiest food in the world. Vital for "super veggie" and longevity. Who doesn't love broccoli!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Water
  • (optional) Salt

Steps

  1. Bring small pot of water to boil on high heat
  2. Add in your broccoli
  3. Turn to medium low heat (4)
  4. Boil for 6 minutes
  5. (optional) Add salt to water
  6. Take out of pot
  7. Serve deliciously!
