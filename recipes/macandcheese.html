Mac and Cheese

Description

A delicious, creamy, and cheesy filled mac and cheese, cooked using macaroni noodle. This mac and cheese also has a delicious added steak, which creates the perfect texture and perfectly tasting dish. The intersection where cheese meets noodles.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • 6 tbsp Butter
  • Sirloin Steak chopped
  • 3 cloves of Minced Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Parsley
  • 4 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 2 cups Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Drain pasta water, then add oil to it to prevent stick
  3. Melt 2 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium
  4. Add steak when butter is barely browned, then cook on medium high
  5. Cook on side for 30 seconds, then flip and cook for 1 min
  6. Melt remaining butter on medium heat then add garlic and parsley, cook 1 min
  7. Add flour and salt, then saute for another minute.
  8. Add milk slowly, continue whisking to incorperate texture
  9. Simmer for 12 minutes, then add cheese
  10. Combine pasta with sauce, then serve warm with steak
