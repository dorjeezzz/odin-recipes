Alfredo

Description:

The recipe is for a quick and classic creamy chicken Aflredo. The Italian pasta is a delicious blend of cheese, chicken, and perfect texture. Good for all meals.

Ingredients

  • Italian Seasoning
  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Season both sides of uncut chicken breast
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium, then add cooking oil
  3. Add chicken to skillet, cook for 8 minutes on medium
  4. Put chicken on side plate
  5. Add butter and garlic, then stir
  6. Add heavy cream after butter melts
  7. Boil the pasta on the side
  8. Drain pasta water when ready, keep 1/2 cup
  9. Add parmesan cheese to skillet, whisk until all sauce has melted, add salt and Pepper
  10. Add pasta to skillet, if sauce is too thick add water
  11. Stir pasta with sauce, add chicken
  12. Serve
